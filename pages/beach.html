Sorted places by traveller's interest

1.KAPPAD BEACH

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Kappad, or Kappakadavu locally, is a beach and village near Koyilandy, in the district Kozhikode, Kerala, India.A stone monument installed by government commemorates the "landing" by Vasco da Gama with the inscription, 'Vasco da Gama landed here, Kappakadavu, in the year 1498'.

2.CALICUT BEACH

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Kozhikode Beach or Calicut Beach is a beach on the western side of Kozhikode, situated on the Malabar Coast of India. The beach is accessible through four road overbridges in the city. The beach has paved stones and illumination.

3.BEYPORE BEACH

⭐⭐⭐⭐

Beypore or Beypur is an ancient port town and a locality town in Kozhikode district in the state of Kerala, India. It is located opposite to Chaliyam, the estuary where the river Chaliyar empties into Arabian Sea.

4.PAYOLI BEACH

⭐⭐⭐

Payyoli beach in Kozhikode is a remote and secluded beach in north Kerala. Take a stroll, mingle with the chirping avian population or even take a relaxing dip. The water, the people and the very earth here, await your presence.

5.MINI-GOA BEACH

⭐⭐⭐⭐

Mini Goa in Calicut: A serene coastal haven reminiscent of its famous counterpart. Golden sands, swaying palms, and tranquil waters await, offering a perfect escape. Enjoy breathtaking sunsets, leisurely walks, and delicious coastal cuisine. Embrace relaxation and nature's beauty at Mini Goa, your seaside sanctuary in Calicut.

6.THANGASSERY BEACH

⭐⭐⭐

Thangassery Beach: A scenic coastal retreat in Kollam, Kerala. With its golden sands, historic lighthouse, and tranquil waters, it's perfect for relaxation and leisurely walks. Enjoy stunning sunsets and explore nearby cultural landmarks for a memorable seaside experience.

7.PARAPPALLY BEACH

⭐⭐⭐

Parapally Beach: A hidden gem along the Malabar Coast in Kerala. Known for its pristine sands, gentle waves, and serene ambiance, it offers a tranquil escape for beach lovers. Explore the quaint surroundings, indulge in fresh seafood delights, and witness breathtaking sunsets for an unforgettable coastal experience.
