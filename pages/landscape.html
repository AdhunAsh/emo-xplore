Sorted places by traveller's interest

1.KARIYATHUMPARA

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Kariyathumpara is a picturesque hill station located in the Kozhikode district of Kerala, India. It's known for its scenic beauty, trekking trails, and lush greenery, making it a popular destination for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.

2.MAVOOR WETLANDS

⭐⭐⭐⭐

Mavoor is a town in the Kozhikode district of Kerala, India. It's known for its historical significance in the region, particularly for its role in the industrial sector, with a focus on industries like tile manufacturing and beedi production.

3.OLOPPARA RICER VIEW POINT

⭐⭐⭐⭐

Oloppara is a beautiful view point at calicut.The ride through the shores of akalappuzha, gives you the sites of mangroove forest on one side and small but beautiful houses on th other side there are some small bamboo huts constructed in to the water.These are not part of tourism but build by the local.

4.KAKKAD FOREST

⭐⭐⭐⭐

Visitors to Kakkad Forest can explore its trails, embark on wildlife safaris, or simply enjoy the serene ambiance of the natural surroundings. It offers opportunities for birdwatching, trekking, and wildlife spotting, allowing visitors to immerse themselves in the beauty of Kerala's wilderness.

5.KADALUNDI BIRD SANCTUARY

⭐⭐⭐⭐

Visitors can explore the sanctuary through guided boat tours, offering opportunities for birdwatching and photography while enjoying the scenic beauty of the estuarine landscape. The tranquil ambiance and abundant birdlife make Kadalundi Bird Sanctuary a must-visit destination for nature enthusiasts and bird lovers alike.

6.SAROVARAM BIO-PARK

⭐⭐⭐⭐

Visitors can explore the park's trails, boardwalks, and watchtowers to observe the rich biodiversity up close. Boating facilities are available for leisurely rides on the lakes, offering serene views of the surrounding landscape. Educational exhibits and interpretation centers provide insights into the region's ecology and conservation efforts.
