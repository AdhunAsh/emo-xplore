Sorted places by traveller's interest

1.KAPPAD BEACH

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Kappad, or Kappakadavu locally, is a beach and village near Koyilandy, in the district Kozhikode, Kerala, India.A stone monument installed by government commemorates the "landing" by Vasco da Gama with the inscription, 'Vasco da Gama landed here, Kappakadavu, in the year 1498'.

2.CALICUT BEACH

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Kozhikode Beach or Calicut Beach is a beach on the western side of Kozhikode, situated on the Malabar Coast of India. The beach is accessible through four road overbridges in the city. The beach has paved stones and illumination.

3.KARIYAMTHUMPARA

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Kariyathumpara is a picturesque hill station located in the Kozhikode district of Kerala, India. It's known for its scenic beauty, trekking trails, and lush greenery, making it a popular destination for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.

4.BEYPORE BEACH

⭐⭐⭐⭐

Beypore or Beypur is an ancient port town and a locality town in Kozhikode district in the state of Kerala, India. It is located opposite to Chaliyam, the estuary where the river Chaliyar empties into Arabian Sea.

5.MANANCHIRA

⭐⭐⭐⭐

Mananchira" refers to a historic place in Kozhikode (Calicut), Kerala, India. Mananchira Square is a popular public space with a large pond surrounded by gardens. It's known for its cultural events, concerts, and as a leisure spot for locals and tourists alike.

6.BUTT-ROAD PARK

⭐⭐⭐⭐

Butt Road Park is a serene urban oasis offering a respite from the city's hustle. With lush greenery, scenic pathways, and recreational facilities, it's an ideal spot for relaxation and leisurely strolls. Escape the bustle and immerse yourself in nature's tranquility at Butt Road Park.

7.SAROVARAM BIO-PARK

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Visitors can explore the park's trails, boardwalks, and watchtowers to observe the rich biodiversity up close. Boating facilities are available for leisurely rides on the lakes, offering serene views of the surrounding landscape. Educational exhibits and interpretation centers provide insights into the region's ecology and conservation efforts.
