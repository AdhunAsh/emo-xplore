Sorted places by traveller's interest

1.MANANCHIRA

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Mananchira" refers to a historic place in Kozhikode (Calicut), Kerala, India. Mananchira Square is a popular public space with a large pond surrounded by gardens. It's known for its cultural events, concerts, and as a leisure spot for locals and tourists alike.

2.BUTT-ROAD PARK

⭐⭐⭐⭐

Butt Road Park is a serene urban oasis offering a respite from the city's hustle. With lush greenery, scenic pathways, and recreational facilities, it's an ideal spot for relaxation and leisurely strolls. Escape the bustle and immerse yourself in nature's tranquility at Butt Road Park.

3.PLANETARIUM

⭐⭐⭐⭐

Planetariums are fascinating places where you can explore the wonders of the universe right here on Earth. They offer captivating shows, educational programs, and a chance to gaze at the stars in a controlled environment. It's like taking a journey through space without leaving your seat!

4.SM STREET

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

SM Street, situated in the heart of Kozhikode, is a bustling hub of activity and culture. Lined with vibrant shops, quaint cafes, and local eateries, it offers a delightful blend of tradition and modernity. From shopping for traditional handicrafts to sampling delicious street food, SM Street is a must-visit destination for tourists and locals alike. Explore the lively atmosphere, soak in the local charm, and indulge in the unique experiences that SM Street has to offer.
