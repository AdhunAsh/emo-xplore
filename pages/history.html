HISTORICAL PLACES AND ART GALLERIES

1.PAZHASSI RAJA ARCHAEOLOGICAL MUSEUM

⭐⭐⭐⭐

Pazhassi Raja Archaeological Museum is a museum and art gallery in Kozhikode, Kerala, India. The museum has a rich collection of historical artifacts from 1000 BC to 200 AD.

2.TALI TEMPLE

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Tali temple is one of the oldest temples in Kozhikode. It is believed that the lingam in the sanctum of the Temple was installed towards the end of Dwaparayuga by Sri. Parasurama.

3.PLANETARIUM

⭐⭐⭐⭐

The Regional Science Centre and Planetarium is one of the most active science centres- is home to six galleries based on Science, Illusion, Life science

4.ART GALLERY

⭐⭐⭐

Art galleries and handicrafts in Kozhikode promote the art and culture of the people residing in the city and nearby areas by displaying their talent.
