Sorted places by traveller's interest

1.PAZHASSI RAJA MUSEUM

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Pazhassi Raja Archaeological Museum is a museum and art gallery in Kozhikode, Kerala, India. The museum has a rich collection of historical artifacts from 1000 BC to 200 AD.

2.KRISHNAMENON MUSEUM

⭐⭐⭐⭐

Krishna Menon Museum in Kozhikode district is dedicated to the late V. K. Krishna Menon, the renowned statesman. The museum has an exclusive section which displays his personal belongings and souvenirs. Adjacent to the museum is a beautiful Art Gallery with an excellent collection of paintings by Raja Ravi Varma and Raja Raja Varma.

3.SARGAALAYA

⭐⭐⭐⭐

Sargaalaya, located in Iringal, Kozhikode, is a unique craft village and cultural center that celebrates the rich tradition of Kerala's arts and crafts. Set amidst serene surroundings, Sargaalaya offers visitors the opportunity to explore and experience various traditional crafts including pottery, handloom weaving, wood carving, and more. The village also features an artisan village where skilled craftsmen showcase their talents and sell their handmade creations. With its vibrant atmosphere and dedication to preserving Kerala's cultural heritage, Sargaalaya serves as a hub for creativity, education, and cultural exchange.

3.ART GALLERY

⭐⭐⭐

Art galleries and handicrafts in Kozhikode promote the art and culture of the people residing in the city and nearby areas by displaying their talent.

4.SK POTTEKKATT MUSEUM

⭐⭐⭐

This is built in the remembrance of prominent writer S. K. Pottekkatt of Malayalam literature hail from Kozhikode.The museum has a great collection of manuscripts , publications journals for display. A small art gallery is also present in the museum literature lovers must definitely visit this place , this place is a huge source of raw information

5.KIRTAD MUSEUM

⭐⭐⭐

The Kerala Institute for Research Training and Development Studies of Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes is an apex institute located in Chevarambalam in Kozhikode district of Kerala. It is a directorate under the Government of Kerala and functions under the SC and ST Department.
