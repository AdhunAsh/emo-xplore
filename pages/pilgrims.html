Sorted places by traveller's interest

1.AZHAKODI TEMPLE

⭐⭐⭐⭐

Azhakodi Devi Temple or Azhakodi Devi Mahakshethram is a Hindu temple located in Thiruthiyad, Kozhikode District, Kerala, South India.

2.CSI CHURCH

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Kozhikode Beach or Calicut Beach is a beach on the western side of Kozhikode, situated on the Malabar Coast of India. The beach is accessible through four road overbridges in the city. The beach has paved stones and illumination.

3.JUMA MASJID

⭐⭐⭐⭐

Situated at Kuttichira in Kozhikode, this mosque can be traced back to 14th century. It has the capacity to accommodate around 1000 worshipers at a time. Built in Kerala style of architecture, it has beautiful wooden carved ceilings. Among the mosques in Kerala, this mosque has the largest floor area and is open to Muslims only.

4.SREEKANTESHWARA TEMPLE

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Sreekanteswara Temple Calicut is in the heart of the city. It is one of the renowned Shiva temples in Kerala. Sreenarayana Guru installed the Shivalingam at the temple in May 1910.

5.MOTHER OF GOD ROMAN CATHOLIC

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Mother of God Cathedral, locally known as Valiya Palli, is situated close to Beach Road in Kozhikode. The church is the headquarters of the Roman Catholic congregation in Malabar. The church has a history dating back to 1513 when the Portuguese signed a treaty with the Zamorins.

6.TALI TEMPLE

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Tali temple is one of the oldest temples in Kozhikode. It is believed that the lingam in the sanctum of the Temple was installed towards the end of Dwaparayuga by Sri. Parasurama.

7.VALAYANAD TEMPLE

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Sree Valayanad Devi Temple dedicated to Bhagavathy, is situated in Valayanad near Kozhikode in North Kerala, India. Valayanad Bhagavathi is the family deity of the Zamorin Kings ( Padinhare Kovilakam), who ruled Kozhikode in the medieval period.
