Sorted places by traveller's interest

1.VAVULMALA

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Vavulmala is a picturesque hill station nestled in the Western Ghats of Kerala, offering breathtaking views and lush greenery. With its tranquil atmosphere and cool climate, it's a popular destination for nature lovers and trekkers. Visitors can embark on scenic hikes through the verdant forests, explore hidden waterfalls, and enjoy panoramic vistas of the surrounding valleys. Vavulmala provides a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of city life, making it an ideal destination for relaxation and rejuvenation amidst nature's beauty.

2.VAYALADA

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Vayalada is a beautiful hill station located near Kakkadampoyil in the Kozhikode district of Kerala, India. It's renowned for its stunning landscapes, trekking trails, and panoramic views of the surrounding hills and valleys. Vayalada is a popular destination for nature lovers and adventurers seeking tranquility amidst nature's bounty.

3.KAKKADAMPOYIL

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Kakkadampoyil is a town located in the Kozhikode district of Kerala, India. It's known for its natural beauty, including lush greenery and serene landscapes.

4.PONPARA KUNNU

⭐⭐⭐⭐

Ponpara Kunnu, nestled in the scenic landscape of Kozhikode, Kerala, is a captivating hilltop destination offering breathtaking views and tranquility. With its lush greenery and serene ambiance, it provides a perfect retreat for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts alike. Visitors can embark on leisurely hikes to the hilltop, where panoramic vistas of the surrounding valleys await. Whether you're seeking solitude or a scenic spot for a picnic, Ponpara Kunnu offers a serene escape amidst nature's beauty.
